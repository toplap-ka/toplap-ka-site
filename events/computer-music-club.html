TOPLAP Karlsruhe

computer_music.club

    time: 20:00 - 03:00 Thursday 22 May 2025
    place: C2 OST Essenweinstraße 9, 76131 Karlsruhe
    tickets: https://c2-ost.club/computer_music-club

On Thursday night, May 22, starting early at 20:00, Karlsruhe becomes the small-scale heaven of club-oriented computer music with amazing new rising forces from LA (c_robo_), Berlin (nesso && bruno gola) and Ljubljana (Blaž Pavlica / cosmic sex) with heavy local support: nervousdata (Freiburg), dkyuh and Michele Samarotto.

From over-saturated digital hardcore to heavily chopped samples and voices, from deconstructed a-cyclic beats to the most elegant single sample feedback, all sounds are live-cooked by the CPUs and their beloved masters.

The night will end with 2 dj sets from cicada and dj Mar with abstract club, dnb and dubstep.

It's danceable, it's always danceable, if you feel it.
