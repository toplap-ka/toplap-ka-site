On Saturday, 21 June, for the 4th edition of Iterative ßpace, we welcome computer musician Naoki Nomoto along with old and new local friends.

Born in Japan 1975, and based in Tokyo, Naoki Nomoto is a self-taught computer/electronic musician and programmer with 20 years of experience in sonic art in Japan. His work explores the intersection of modular synthesis and algorithmic composition, using SuperCollider alongside modular systems.

Iterative ßpace is a concert series in Karlsruhe featuring experimental algorithmic and live computer music. It started in 2023 with a focus on live coding and aims to include diverse practices of electronic music and sound performance.
