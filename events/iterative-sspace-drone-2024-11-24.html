iterative ßpace #3: drrrrone edition

  • ßpace
    Fritz-Erler-Straße 7, 76133 Karlsruhe
    November 24th 2024, 15:00

It's getting darker, and we need music.
On Sunday afternoon, 24.11, we invite you to lie down in ßpace and have a sonic bath with live, intense drone music.
You may close your eyes or watch the projection on the ceiling — the magic of live computational synthesis and the process of creation will be revealed.

Line-up:
Daniel Höpfner, Michele Samarotto, Anselm Weber.

Iterative ßpace is a concert series featuring experimental algorithmic and live computer music, where artistic thinking unfolds through the coding process.
For more information, visit https://toplap-ka.de.
